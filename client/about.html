About Lonestar Autos

Your trusted partner for premium vehicles and nationwide delivery

Our Story

Founded in 2019 in the heart of Texas, Lonestar Autos began with a simple mission: to redefine the car buying experience by combining luxury vehicles with transparent pricing and hassle-free nationwide delivery. What started as a small family-owned dealership has grown into a trusted name across the United States, serving over 500 satisfied customers and counting.

We believe that buying a dream car should be an exciting journey, not a complicated transaction. That's why we've built a business model centered on trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes rigorous multi-point inspection by ASE-certified technicians, ensuring you drive away with confidence.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us a 4.9/5 customer rating and recognition as a Texas Best Dealership finalist in 2023. We're proud to be state-approved and fully licensed to operate across all 50 states.
