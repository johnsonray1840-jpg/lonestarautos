Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about vehicle shipping

How is shipping cost calculated?
Shipping costs are based on two main factors: your vehicle type (size/weight) and delivery distance. Larger vehicles and longer distances increase the cost. Use our shipping calculator above to get an estimate.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5-10 business days nationwide. Express delivery is available for 2-3 business days. Delivery times may vary based on weather, traffic, and other factors.
Is my vehicle insured during transport?
Yes! All vehicles shipped through our partners are fully insured for the full value of the vehicle. You'll receive insurance documentation with your shipping confirmation.
Can I track my vehicle in real-time?
Absolutely! Once your vehicle is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor your delivery in real-time via our tracking page. GPS updates are provided every 30 minutes.
What if my vehicle is damaged during shipping?
In the rare event of damage, our insurance coverage protects you. Document any damage upon delivery and contact us within 48 hours to file a claim.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We offer international shipping to select countries. Contact our support team for a custom quote and shipping timeline.
