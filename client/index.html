Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and shipping with Lonestar Autos

How does vehicle shipping work?
After purchasing your vehicle, our team coordinates with our shipping partners to arrange delivery. You'll receive a tracking number and real-time updates. Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and vehicle type. Most deliveries take 3-10 business days depending on distance.
How is shipping cost calculated?
Shipping costs depend on two main factors: your vehicle type (size/weight) and delivery distance. When you proceed to checkout, you'll enter your delivery address and vehicle details to get an accurate shipping estimate. Final costs are confirmed before payment.
Can I finance my vehicle purchase?
Yes! We offer flexible financing options for qualified buyers. You can request financing during checkout by selecting the "Request Financing" option. Our finance partners work with all credit types and will contact you within 24 hours with approval details.
What is the reservation process?
You can reserve any vehicle with a 10% down payment. This holds the vehicle for 7 days while you complete financing or arrange full payment. If you change your mind, you have 48 hours for a full refund. The remaining balance is due upon delivery.
Is my vehicle insured during transport?
Absolutely! All vehicles shipped through our partners are fully insured for the full value of the vehicle. You'll receive insurance documentation with your shipping confirmation.
What payment methods do you accept?
For down payments and reservations, we accept PayPal, Venmo, and Cash App. For full payments, wire transfer is available. Card payments are temporarily unavailable due to enhanced security measures.
